How to Extract Your OMR Data

Follow these steps to automatically download your OMR responses as a CSV file, ready to be uploaded here.

Method 1: The Bookmarklet (PC / Laptop)

1. Drag the blue button below into your browser's Bookmarks Bar (Press Ctrl+Shift+B to show the bar).

📥 Drag to Bookmarks

2. Go to the Official NTA "View Recorded Responses" webpage.
3. Click the bookmark you just saved. It will ask for your name and download your CSV!

Method 2: Console Script (Mobile / Fallback)

If you are on a phone, copy the code below. Go to the NTA OMR webpage, type javascript: in the URL bar, paste the code right after it, and hit enter/go.
