Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Your privacy is absolute. This policy explains how data is handled on our platform.

1. OMR CSV Data Processing

When you upload your OMR response CSV file, all scoring calculations happen entirely inside your own web browser. We do not upload, store, or view your raw response selections on any external server.

2. Global Leaderboard Data

To compute percentiles, the tool automatically securely saves your extracted masked initials (e.g., A**** K****), your total marks, and subject subtotals to our secure database. Your application numbers are hashed and used solely as system unique identifiers to prevent duplicate entry manipulation; they are never publicly displayed or exposed.

3. Third-Party Payments

Voluntary support contributions are processed securely through Razorpay. We do not store or collect your banking, card, or UPI credentials.
